About Me

Originating from the humble town of Tayabas City, Leonardo III M. Calupig is a 20 year old undergraduate student that is a celebrated editor and cinematographer. He is currently pursuing his Bachelor’s degree in Entertainment and Multimedia Computing (BSEMC) Major in Digital Animation Technology (DAT) at Manuel S. Enverga University Foundation. He once studied Civil Engineering but eventually shifted focus in his passion and commitment to push the boundaries of storytelling. He is the visionary mind who captured the story behind the award winning short film “Per Aspera Ad Astra” which won at the 2024 MSEUF M.I.L. Film Festival: “Breaking the Silence: Short Film Making on Mental Heath.” His commitment to his edited works are a rollercoaster of suspense, elegance on a endless journey into the realms of both real and digital worlds.
